# Product Definition: Passion Seed

## Initial Concept
Passion Seed is an AI-driven career path simulation and exploration platform designed specifically for students and early-career professionals. It leverages data-rich analysis of job markets and educational programs to provide a gamified, interactive discovery experience.

## Vision
To empower the next generation of professionals to discover and navigate their ideal career paths with confidence and clarity through data-driven simulations and personalized AI guidance.

## Target Audience
- **Students/Graduates:** Individuals currently in education or recently graduated who are exploring potential career directions.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Path Simulation:** A unique, AI-powered system that allows users to simulate and explore different career trajectories, understanding the requirements and outcomes of various paths before committing to them.

## Key Features & Experience
- **Gamified Discovery:** An interactive, game-like interface that makes career exploration engaging and intuitive.
- **Data-Rich Analysis:** Deep integration with job market data and university program information to ground simulations in reality.
- **Guided Reflection:** Personalized AI coaching and reflection tools to help users process their findings and refine their goals.

## Strategic Priority
- **Mobile-First:** A seamless, high-performance mobile application experience is the primary focus, optimized for on-the-go exploration and interaction.

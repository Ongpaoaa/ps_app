# Product Guidelines: Passion Seed

## Branding & Voice
- **Tone: The Mentor.** Our voice is friendly, encouraging, and supportive. We aim to be a helpful guide, not an authoritative judge. We use clear, accessible language and provide positive reinforcement throughout the career exploration journey.
- **Visual Identity:** Our visual style is clean and modern, prioritizing clarity and reducing cognitive load. We use a sophisticated but accessible color palette that conveys both professionalism and approachability.

## User Experience (UX) Principles
- **Clarity & Simplicity:** Every screen should have a clear purpose. We minimize distractions and use intuitive navigation to help users focus on their career discovery.
- **Efficiency-First:** We value our users' time. Workflows are designed to be direct and fast, allowing power users to navigate complex career data and simulations with ease.
- **Interactive Feedback:** Actions should have clear, immediate visual or haptic feedback to reinforce the feeling of an interactive, responsive experience.

## Design Patterns
- **Card-Based Layouts:** Use cards to encapsulate career paths, university programs, and individual steps in a journey, making information digestible and easily comparable.
- **Progressive Disclosure:** Show only the most relevant information initially, allowing users to dive deeper into data-rich simulations as needed.
- **Accessible Typography:** Prioritize readability across all devices, ensuring high contrast and appropriate font sizes for a mobile-first audience.
